Louis Theroux says he reached ‘lowest point’ during Manosphere documentary
- Louis Theroux has provided a health update regarding his five-year battle with alopecia.
- He revealed that his autoimmune condition was at its worst during the filming of his Netflix documentary, 'Inside the Manosphere'.
- Theroux noted that viewers might have observed his hair appearing 'quite weird' and 'patchy' in certain scenes of the documentary.
- Despite the previous severity, he shared positive news that his hair has begun to grow back.
- He expressed uncertainty about the reason for his hair regrowth, attributing it to the 'vague mysteries of life and the human condition'.
