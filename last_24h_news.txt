Alyssa Farah Griffin calls Donald Trump Jr.’s wedding a ‘funeral’ in on-air gaffe
- A host on The View, Alyssa Farah Griffin, accidentally referred to Donald Trump Jr.'s wedding as a "funeral" during a live broadcast.
- The gaffe happened while the presenters were discussing Donald Trump's absence from his son's wedding.
- Donald Trump chose to stay in Washington DC rather than attend his son's marriage to socialite Bettina Anderson in the Bahamas.
- Griffin, who previously worked as Trump's White House strategic communications director, quickly corrected her mistake.
- She explained her slip-up by stating she was tired, mentioning she has a three-month-old baby.

Police in ‘hop pursuit’ of runaway kangaroo through Texas neighbourhood
- Police in Texas pursued an escaped kangaroo after it fled from Waco Wildlife Rescue.
- Footage shared by the Waco Police Department on Tuesday, 26 May, showed officers chasing the marsupial.
- The kangaroo was safely captured after a brief pursuit, with officers restraining it by its tail.
- It was returned to the wildlife rescue without any injuries to the animal or the officers involved.
- This incident marks the second time in a year that a marsupial has escaped from the Waco Wildlife Rescue, following a previous breakout of two kangaroos in September.
