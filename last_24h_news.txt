Madonna offers reward as historic Coachella performance outfit vanishes
- Pop superstar Madonna has reported that the outfit she wore during her surprise appearance at Coachella has gone missing.
- The costume, which included a jacket, corset, and dress, was taken from her personal archives, along with other archival items from the same era.
- Madonna announced the disappearance on Instagram Stories, appealing to the public for assistance in recovering the items.
- She is offering a reward for the safe return of the garments, emphasising their significance as part of her history.
- Madonna joined Sabrina Carpenter on stage at Coachella, performing classic hits and a new song, marking a 'full circle' moment as she had performed at the festival 20 years prior.

Donald Trump condemned for using Frank Sinatra song on social media
- Nancy Sinatra, daughter of the late Frank Sinatra, has condemned Donald Trump for using her father's music.
- Trump shared a clip of Frank Sinatra performing “My Way” on Truth Social, which Nancy Sinatra labelled a “sacrilege”.
- She stated that she is unable to take legal action against Trump, as only the music publishers have that authority.
- Nancy Sinatra has repeatedly claimed that her father 'loathed' Trump, a sentiment she has expressed since 2017.
- Frank Sinatra's former manager, Eliot Weisman, and ex-wife, Mia Farrow, have also corroborated his strong dislike for Trump.
