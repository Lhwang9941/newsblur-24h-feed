Led by Donkeys ‘rename’ Larry Ellison’s $160 million yacht in Riviera stunt
A protest group has “renamed” billionaire Larry Ellison’s super yacht to “The Trump Propagandist”.
Footage released by Led By Donkeys on Tuesday (24 March) shows activists approaching Ellison’s $160 million yacht in the French Riviera by speedboat before “redecorating” the 280-foot vessel.
“We tracked down the superyacht owned by the world’s sixth-richest man. Larry Ellison won’t like what we did to it,” the group said.
Ellison, co-founder of software company Oracle, is known for his ties to Donald Trump and Benjamin Netanyahu. In 2017, he made what was then the largest single donation to the Israeli Defense Forces, contributing $16.6 million.
