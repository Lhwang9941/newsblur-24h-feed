Zelensky’s office confirms next round of talks on February 17–18 in Geneva
Ukrinform
Ukraine’s presidential communications advisor Dmytro Lytvyn confirmed that the next round of negotiations between Ukraine, the United States, and Russia will take place in Geneva on February 17–18.
According to an Ukrinform correspondent, Lytvyn provided the confirmation to journalists in response to a question about whether the talks would occur in Geneva on those dates.
“Yes, as of today, the Ukrainian delegation is preparing,” he said.
As previously reported, media outlets had indicated that the next round of trilateral talks between Ukraine, the U.S., and Russia would be held in Geneva on February 17–18.
