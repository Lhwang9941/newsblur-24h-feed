Women in Defence Charter

The Women in Defence Charter was launched to improve gender balance in the defence sector, both public and private.
By signing, organisations pledge to be the very best at driving inclusion and diversity, providing fair opportunities for women to succeed at all levels.

Terrorism investigation launched after suspect hurls ‘incendiary device’ at ICE office in NYC
26 Federal Plaza is a federal building located in downtown Manhattan that houses an ICE Field Office and immigration courts

The New York Joint Terrorism Task Force took a man into custody Monday morning after he allegedly ignited “an incendiary device” outside of a federal building that houses Immigration and Customs Enforcement offices.
Police apprehended the man, who has not been publicly identified, after he set off the device in front of 26 Federal Plaza in Manhattan around 8:30 a.m., according to a video posted on X by Freedom News TV.
The Independent has asked the FBI New York Office for comment.
