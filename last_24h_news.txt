I’m a Celebrity star admits regret over David Haye clash that ‘crossed a line’
- Adam Thomas, a contestant on I'm A Celebrity: South Africa, revealed that fellow campmate David Haye “broke” him during their time on the show, pushing him to his limits.
- Thomas, who was diagnosed with psoriatic arthritis in 2023, stated that his autoimmune illness took its toll in the jungle, and he tried to hide his pain.
- Haye drew criticism for calling Thomas “useless” after he declined a Bushtucker Trial due to feeling unwell, and also for penalising him over sneaking in chocolate.
- In an Instagram post on Sunday (19 April), Thomas shared his emotional struggles, expressing regret for not speaking up sooner but confirming he has since processed the experience and accepted Haye's apology.
- Presenters Ant and Dec also commented on Haye's behaviour towards Thomas, describing it as “not a nice watch” and suggesting it crossed the line from banter.
